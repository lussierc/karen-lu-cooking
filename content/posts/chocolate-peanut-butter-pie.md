Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie

 For best results make sure ingredients are room temperature and make a day in advance.

*Ingredients:*

* *Oreo Cookie or Chocolate pie crust*
* 3/4 cups margarine (room temp)
* 1/2 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
* 3/4 cup peanut butter
* 5 cups (12 ounces) Cool Whip (regular or lite) (room temp)
* 2 squares semisweet chocolate (Bakers)
* 1 additional square semisweet chocolate to top
* cool whip to top

Directions:

Beat margarine, brown sugar and peanut butter in mixer on a creamy beat or high speed until the mixture is smooth and not grainy, about 5 minutes.  Stir in whipped cream until well blended and add in 2 squares of melted chocolate until well blended.  Spoon into pie crust and drizzle with remaining melted chocolate.  Refrigerate at least 4 hours but 24 hours for best results.

Note- follow directions on back of box to melt chocolate in microwave
